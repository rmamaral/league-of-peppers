About the League of Legends pick generator

League of Peppers is a free League of Legends champion generator for players who are bored of the same meta picks. Hit the button and get an instant recommendation: an off-meta (“spicy”) or classic solo pick, a synergized bot-lane duo, or a full five-champion team comp. Every roll comes with a suggested item build, a rune page, a skill-max order, and a clear win condition, so you know not just what to pick but how to play it.

Champion portraits, items, and runes are pulled live from Riot’s Data Dragon, so builds always match the current League of Legends patch. There are 150+ curated picks, 15 or more for every role (Top, Jungle, Mid, Bot, Support) in both spicy and classic flavors - plus dozens of bot-lane duos and fifteen full team comps with a breakdown of how they win.

Frequently asked questions

What is an off-meta or “spicy” pick in League of Legends?
A champion played in a role or style that isn’t standard for the current meta, like an AP carry bot lane or a mage in the jungle. Unconventional, but still viable, and it catches opponents off guard.
Are the builds and runes up to date?
Yes. Items and runes come live from Data Dragon, so they always reflect the latest League of Legends patch. The curated picks and identity builds are hand-made and reviewed regularly.
Does the generator cover every role?
Yes, 15+ picks for every role in both spicy and classic flavors, plus a bot-lane duo generator, a full team-comp generator, a team analyzer, and a draft simulator.
Is League of Peppers free?
Completely free, with no sign-up. Roll as many League of Legends picks, duos, and comps as you want.
