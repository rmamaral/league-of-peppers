Team comps

Full team comps

🎯 Poke & Siege

Champions: Jayce (Top), Nidalee (Jungle), Xerath (Mid), Ziggs (Bot), Karma (Support)

Long-range damage that whittles the enemy down before a fight even starts, then takes towers while they’re too low to contest.

Power spike: Mid game

Win condition: Chip them down from max range, take towers for free, and never let them force an engage.

How to play: Shove waves and poke them off the objective; Take towers and dragons with the health lead; Never facecheck — one landed engage and you lose the fight.

Countered by: Hard engage and flanks. One Malphite ult on the squishy backline erases the comp.

💥 Wombo Dive

Champions: Malphite (Top), Amumu (Jungle), Orianna (Mid), Aphelios (Bot), Rell (Support)

Stacked AOE ultimates that chain into one unstoppable teamfight combo. Land it once and the game is over.

Power spike: Mid–late (once ultimates are up)

Win condition: One good engage ends the game. Land the chain of ults and ace them in four seconds.

How to play: Group and force fights around objectives; Find a flank or flash-engage to open; Call ults in order: engage → follow-up → clean-up.

Countered by: Disengage, poke, and split-push. If they scatter before the combo lands, you have nothing.

🛡️ Protect the Carry

Champions: Shen (Top), Ivern (Jungle), Orianna (Mid), Kog'Maw (Bot), Lulu (Support)

One hyper-scaling carry and four champions whose entire job is to keep them alive long enough to 1v9.

Power spike: Late game

Win condition: Everything exists to keep Kog’Maw alive. Peel, shield, and let him melt them front-to-back.

How to play: Survive early and farm the carry to full items; In fights, body-block and peel — never trade the carry; Let the enemy commit, then melt them down.

Countered by: Assassins and hard dive that leap past the peel, and getting run over before the carry comes online.

🎣 Pick Comp

Champions: Camille (Top), Nocturne (Jungle), Ahri (Mid), Jhin (Bot), Thresh (Support)

Mobility and crowd control built to catch one target and delete them before a real 5v5 ever happens.

Power spike: Mid game

Win condition: Catch one target out of position, turn every fight into a 5v4, and snowball the map.

How to play: Control vision and lurk around choke points; Force a pick, then collapse for the 5v4; Convert the numbers advantage into objectives.

Countered by: Grouped, disciplined teams that never split or facecheck. No picks means no plan.

🗺️ Split Push 1-3-1

Champions: Fiora (Top), Nidalee (Jungle), Twisted Fate (Mid), Sivir (Bot), Tahm Kench (Support)

Duelists on the side lanes that pressure the map while global ults hold the middle and answer any fight.

Power spike: Mid–late

Win condition: Pressure both side lanes. When they collapse on Fiora, TF/Sivir ult the 4v4 mid and take the map.

How to play: Send strong duelists to opposite side lanes; Trade objectives — take theirs while they answer your splitter; Use TF/global ults to swing any skirmish.

Countered by: Hard engage onto your mid group, and teams that 5-man dive the splitter with numbers.

🧱 Front-to-Back

Champions: Ornn (Top), Sejuani (Jungle), Orianna (Mid), Aphelios (Bot), Braum (Support)

An immovable tank wall in front and a hyper-carry safely dealing damage behind it. You simply out-last them.

Power spike: Late game

Win condition: Stack tanks, walk at them, and let Aphelios delete their team from behind an unbreakable wall.

How to play: Stack tanks and walk at objectives together; Absorb their engage on the frontline; Let the carry auto them down from safety — never chase.

Countered by: Poke comps that chip you before you arrive, and flanks that skip the frontline entirely.

🌍 Global Pressure
